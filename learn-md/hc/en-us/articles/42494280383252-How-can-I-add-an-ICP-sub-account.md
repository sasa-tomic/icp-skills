# How can I add an ICP (sub-)account?

Logging into the NNS dapp using an Internet Identity creates a [main account](/hc/en-us/articles/42493986016788) for you in the ICP ledger.

You can create one or more linked *sub-accounts* or attach a hardware wallet to your account.

#### Step 1: Go on the 'Tokens' tab in the left menu.

![](/hc/article_attachments/42494309267860)

#### Step 2: In the top table, select 'Internet Computer'.

![](/hc/article_attachments/42494280371732)

#### Step 3: Select 'Add Account'.

![](/hc/article_attachments/42494309272084)

#### Step 4: Select the type of account to add, which is 'New Account'.

- **New Account** creates a new subaccount linked to your Main account address in the ledger.
- **New Account (Hardware Wallet)** adds a hardware wallet to your main account address in the ledger.

#### Step 5: Enter an account name and hit 'Create'.

![](/hc/article_attachments/42494309273492)

#### You now have a new account with this name in your list.

![](/hc/article_attachments/42494309276564)
